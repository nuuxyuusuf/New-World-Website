Articles on Democracy

The Significance of Democracy and Social Capital (Putnam, 2017)

The significance of democracy extends beyond national borders, influencing global interactions and relationships...

Putnam defines social capital as the networks, norms, and trust that help people cooperate and build strong communities...

He warns that the decline of social capital weakens democracy, reducing civic engagement and trust...

However, he believes it can be revived through community programs, education, and shared public spaces.

Leadership Challenges in Democratic Societies (Smith, 2020)

While democracy supports empowerment and stability, it can also lead to weak leadership and poor governance...

Smith explains that leaders face pressure from media, public opinion, and the need to balance competing interests...

He highlights the importance of accountability and informed citizens in maintaining effective governance...

Smith suggests reforms like civic education and fairer elections to improve leadership quality in democratic societies.
